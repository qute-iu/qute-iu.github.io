QuTE aims to provide skills, platform and recognition to highly motivated students excited by the advancement of quantum computing. QuTE is interdisciplinary and currently has a student body from Kelley, Luddy, Jacobs and Arts and Sciences. We are closely linked with QISE program (https://qsec.sitehost.iu.edu/) from IU QSEc and have a network of over 30 companies. Everyone is welcome.
