Statement of Purpose

Quantum Technologies for Everyone is a student run organization for bringing together people interested in quantum information science from a variety of academic fields and disciplines. We are a team comprised of students that represent the growing IU Quantum Science and Engineering Center. Our mission is to equip the future quantum workforce with the skills necessary in the field of QIS. Furthermore, we strive to make industrial connections and remain on the cutting edge of quantum information science and engineering. We are closely linked with QISE program (https://qsec.sitehost.iu.edu/) from IU QSEc and have a network of over 30 companies. Everyone is welcome.
