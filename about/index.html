Meet Our Executive Board

Our executive board is comprised of graduate and undergraduate students pursuing QIS from different corners of IU's Quantum Science and Engineering Center. It is our goal to provide the club with interesting events and a strong QIS community here at Indiana University. Many of the clubs more technial pursuits are going to depend largely on the collective interest from the majority of our active club members. To find more detailed information on club proceedings, see QuTE Constitution and Guidelines.
