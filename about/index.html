Executive Board

Our executive board is comprised of graduate and undergraduate students pursuing QIS from very differrent areas of expertise. It is our goal to provide the club with interesting events and a strong Quantum Information Science community here at Indiana University. Many of the clubs more technial pursuits are going to depend largely on the collective interest from the majority of our active club members. For more detailed information, see QuTE Constitution and Guidelines.
