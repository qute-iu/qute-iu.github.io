  • April-17-2024: Measurment induced heating paper on arXive!

    • Find arXive link here. Heating is detrimental to the operation of trapped ion quantum computing hardware. A most well known source of this heating, often reffered to as "anamolous heating", heavily dictaces the entirety of the trap design and fabrication process. This paper is a study from a different kind of heating source, which is induced by the measurement of a trapped ion quantum state.
